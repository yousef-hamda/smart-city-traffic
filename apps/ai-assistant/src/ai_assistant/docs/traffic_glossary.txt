Jerusalem Traffic Glossary — English / Hebrew / Arabic

1. Traffic Jam / פקק תנועה / ازدحام مروري
   A situation where vehicles are stopped or moving very slowly due to high density.

2. Congestion / עומס תנועה / ازدحام
   Increased vehicle density on a road causing reduced speeds and longer travel times.

3. Speed Limit / מגבלת מהירות / حد السرعة
   Maximum legal speed on a road segment, measured in km/h.

4. Rush Hour / שעת שיא / ساعة الذروة
   Peak traffic periods, typically 07:00–09:30 and 16:00–19:00 in Jerusalem.

5. Incident / תקרית / حادثة
   An event affecting traffic flow: accident, vehicle breakdown, debris on road.

6. Road Closure / סגירת כביש / إغلاق طريق
   Full or partial blockage of a road segment to traffic.

7. Detour / עקיפה / مسار بديل
   Alternative route taken to avoid congestion or a closure.

8. Bottleneck / צוואר בקבוק / نقطة اختناق
   A narrow section of road where capacity is lower than demand, causing backups.

9. Throughput / תפוקה / الإنتاجية
   Number of vehicles passing a point per unit time (veh/hour).

10. Intersection / צומת / تقاطع
    A junction where two or more roads meet or cross.

11. Traffic Light / רמזור / إشارة ضوئية
    Automated signal controlling vehicle flow at an intersection.

12. Green Wave / גל ירוק / موجة خضراء
    Coordination of traffic signals to allow continuous flow along a corridor.

13. Flow Rate / ספיקת תנועה / معدل التدفق
    Volume of traffic passing a sensor, measured in vehicles per minute.

14. Occupancy / תפוסה / الإشغال
    Percentage of time a road section is occupied by vehicles.

15. Free-Flow Speed / מהירות חופשית / سرعة التدفق الحر
    Speed at which vehicles travel when there is no congestion.

16. Level of Service (LOS) / רמת שירות / مستوى الخدمة
    A–F scale measuring road performance; A = free flow, F = breakdown.

17. Sensor / חיישן / مستشعر
    A device measuring traffic parameters: loop detector, camera, radar.

18. Road Segment / קטע כביש / مقطع طريق
    A defined portion of a road between two junctions or waypoints.

19. Shortest Path / מסלול קצר ביותר / أقصر مسار
    The route minimising travel time or distance between origin and destination.

20. Reachability / נגישות / إمكانية الوصول
    Whether a destination can be reached from an origin given current network state.

21. Edge Weight / משקל קשת / وزن الحافة
    Travel time or distance assigned to a road edge in the graph model.

22. Pedestrian Zone / אזור להולכי רגל / منطقة المشاة
    A street section restricted from motor vehicle traffic.

23. Bus Lane / נתיב אוטובוס / مسرب الحافلات
    A dedicated lane reserved for public buses, sometimes taxis.

24. Light Rail / רכבת קלה / القطار الخفيف
    Jerusalem's tram system running primarily along Jaffa Road.

25. Parking Congestion / פקק חניה / ازدحام موقف السيارات
    Traffic backups caused by vehicles searching for or queuing for parking.
